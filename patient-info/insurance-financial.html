Insurance & Financial Policies

Insurance Information

We understand that navigating dental insurance can be confusing. Our team is here to help you understand your benefits and maximize your coverage.

  • Dental insurance differs from medical insurance in coverage structure
  • Please review your insurance benefit booklet for specific details
  • Patient payment portion varies depending on your coverage
  • We provide an insurance estimate at treatment time (this is an estimate, not a guarantee)
  • Our office files insurance claims as a courtesy to our patients
  • You are responsible for any balance if insurance pays less than estimated
  • Credit will be issued for any overpayment

Financial Policy

We accept: Cash, debit cards, Visa, MasterCard, Discover, and American Express.

CareCredit Financing

For procedures not fully covered by insurance, we offer CareCredit financing with low minimum monthly payment options and an instant approval process. CareCredit can help make dental care more affordable.

Cancellation Policy

We ask that you provide at least 48 hours notice if you need to reschedule your appointment. Appointments cancelled with less than 24 hours notice may incur a cancellation charge. We understand that emergencies happen, so please call us as soon as possible if you need to make changes.
