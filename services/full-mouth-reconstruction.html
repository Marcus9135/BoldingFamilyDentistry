Full Mouth Reconstruction

Full Mouth Reconstruction is a multi-step process that can improve both the health and the appearance of your smile by utilizing a variety of dental procedures and treatments.

Bolding Family Dentistry provides full mouth reconstruction in Columbia, TN. Call 931-388-2279 to learn more and schedule an appointment.

What is Full Mouth Reconstruction?

A full mouth reconstruction is a personalized treatment plan that combines multiple restorative and cosmetic dental procedures to rebuild or replace all of the teeth in your mouth. This comprehensive approach addresses severe dental issues, such as extensive tooth decay, widespread tooth loss, significant damage from trauma, or advanced gum disease. The goal is to create a healthy, functional, and aesthetically pleasing smile.

Benefits of Full Mouth Reconstruction

  • Improved Oral Health and Function. By addressing underlying issues like decay, gum disease, and missing teeth, this process eliminates pain and infection. It restores your ability to chew properly and speak clearly, which can significantly improve digestion and nutrition.
  • Enhanced Comfort and Confidence. Chronic dental problems often cause discomfort and self-consciousness. A full mouth reconstruction resolves these issues, giving you a beautiful, natural-looking smile you can be proud of. This boost in confidence can positively impact your social and professional life.
  • Long-Term Durability. The materials and techniques used in modern restorative dentistry are designed for longevity. Procedures like dental implants and crowns provide a strong, stable, and durable foundation that can last for many years with proper care, protecting your investment in your oral health.

Services or Procedures We Offer

Your full mouth reconstruction plan is tailored to your unique needs. It may include a combination of the following procedures:

Smile Makeovers

Are You a Good Candidate for Full Mouth Reconstruction?

You may be a good candidate for full mouth reconstruction if you have multiple, complex dental issues. This can include numerous missing or broken teeth, severe decay, advanced periodontal disease, or significant bite problems. The best way to determine if this is the right path for you is to schedule a comprehensive evaluation with Dr. Bolding.

Frequently Asked Questions About Full Mouth Reconstruction

What is the difference between a smile makeover and a full mouth reconstruction?

A smile makeover is an elective cosmetic procedure focused on improving the appearance of your smile. A full mouth reconstruction is a necessary process focused on restoring the health and function of your teeth and jaw, though it also provides significant cosmetic benefits.

How long does the process take?

The timeline varies depending on the complexity of your case and the specific procedures involved. Some treatments may be completed in a few visits, while others involving implants or gum therapy can take several months to a year. We will provide a detailed timeline during your consultation.

Is full mouth reconstruction painful?

We prioritize your comfort throughout the process. Anesthetics and sedation options are used to ensure procedures are painless. Some tenderness or soreness is normal after certain treatments, but we will provide instructions to manage any discomfort.

How much does full mouth reconstruction cost?

The cost is unique to each patient's treatment plan. After your initial examination, we will provide a detailed breakdown of all associated costs and review your insurance coverage and financing options with you.

How do I care for my smile after reconstruction?

Maintaining your new smile requires excellent oral hygiene, including daily brushing and flossing. Regular check-ups and cleanings at our office are also essential to ensure the long-term success and durability of your restorations.
