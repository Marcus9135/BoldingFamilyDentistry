Same-Day Crowns

Same Day Restoration Technology

Same Day Restoration technology fabricates durable and aesthetic dental restorations such as crowns and bridges.

Depending on the procedure(s) being performed, your dental restoration(s) may be able to be placed on the same day or the next day instead of the usual wait of one to two weeks or more.

Bolding Family Dentistry provides same day crowns in Columbia, TN. Call 931-388-2279 to learn more and schedule an appointment.

How It Works

Using a digital scanner, our team can create a digital impression of your teeth and gums without using traditional trays, improving patient comfort. Your custom restoration(s) are then designed using advanced 3D computer technology that can analyze the interaction between the new restoration and your other teeth.

The new crown or bridge is milled from tooth-colored composite material, providing strength that may surpass that of many crowns produced in a dental lab. Advancements in materials and technology have provided many options for high-quality restorations that can both look great and provide superior strength compared to other restorations, while reducing the need for multiple visits to the office.
