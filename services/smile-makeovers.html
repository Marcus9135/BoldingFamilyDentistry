Smile Makeovers

Smile Makeovers in Columbia

A smile makeover is a multi-step process involving many different types of cosmetic dentistry procedures to improve both the health and the appearance of your smile. The specific order of treatments will be outlined and explained during the evaluation. At the end of this process, your mouth will look and feel fabulous!

Bolding Family Dentistry provides smile makeovers in Columbia, TN. Call 931-388-2279 to learn more and schedule an appointment.

Your smile makeover may include any of the following treatments:

Tooth-colored Fillings

Dental Crowns

Dental Bridges

Dental Veneers

Dental Implants

Tooth Bonding

Orthodontics

Periodontal Treatment
